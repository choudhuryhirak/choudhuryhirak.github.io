How does it work?

When you are pregnant, some of your baby’s DNA circulates in your blood stream. Claria NIPT is a simple and safe non-invasive screening test that analyzes this DNA to determine your baby’s risk for a number of genetic disorders such as Down Syndrome, Patau syndrome and many others including microdeletions.

NIPT Test Process - What to Expect

The process is simple. After discussing with our certified genetics expert, he/she will help you find the closest sample collection center.
A small sample of blood is collected from the mother's arm and therefore poses no risk of a miscarriage. Within fifteen days of sample collection you will receive your Claria NIPT report, and you can consult with your doctor.
