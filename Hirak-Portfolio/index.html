About


Me

Here's the boring bio stuff, in case you're interested.

I'm still in that paradigm where I consider myself a designer as well as a developer. Growing up, I was always fascinated by cartoons, comics, and video games, probably that was the reason why I chose to specialize in multimedia at grad school. After graduation, I set off to the United Kingdom to obtain my master’s degree on Interactive Media.It was here I realized that I’m way too interested in the technical part of building applications, I wanted to know how apps actually work, so I gravitated towards the “development side of the force” [--the force of all things software if you consider design and development to be separate sides.]

It's been a year since I obtained my master’s degree. I’ve been honing my skills as a developer as well as an UX Designer. I feel users are often neglected before most applications are ideated. This, in turn, results in products which are half finished and one-dimensional. I’ve worked at Fairfest Media a company based in Kolkata, and they provided me the opportunity to look into some real use cases of imperfect User Experiences. We built an awesome hotel booking app called SelectRooms and also discussed the strategies of redesigning some of their websites.

Soon, I felt it was time for me to move on to polish my skills in Front End Web Development and video games development.I sort of felt the need to learn more and design more. These days you can find me busy learning or coding on my new Predator workstation and occassionally handling few graphic design challenges. And of course this site is for you, the users. If you have any interesting projects in mind and you feel I'm the right fit, do not hesitate to contact me.

UX Research

Information Architectue arrow_right Layouts arrow_right Clickable Prototypes arrow_right Storyboards

Every idea needs a proper prototype.As a building cannot be made without a proper foundation, a product needs proper planning and research before it can be created. Before spending valuable resources on websites and apps you would require a usability review of your competitors and it's my job to make sure that you don't make the same mistakes as them. I'll help you identify your buissness goals and help you make a product that not only looks great, but aligns your proto-assumptions with real user-expectations.


Interface Design

Design Strategy arrow_right Branding arrow_right Interface Identity

So after the research process is done, we’ll have with us an MVP(Minimal Viable Prototype). We’ll have to go that extra mile now to make sure your product engages with the user.Its time now to move to the drawing boards and create the best interface for your product. Its important that the design is user-centric and connects with users at an emotional level. I’ll also make sure that your product is a part of your brand while maintaining Design Integrity.
