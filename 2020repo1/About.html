Hi everyone, I'm Supriya Sahai

Why is it so easy for most of us to live a life of unfulfillment? It is easy for us to keep brooding about the many things that you have been wanting to do and how there have been so many valid and rational reasons of not being able to do so. All of us go through the phase of supressing what truly drives us. We call this being "rational".Slowly and steadily we notice that we have stopped living in the need of being "rational". It feels like the rat on the hampster. All we need in such moments is to really be bold and look out for the next step to explore within with a coach - someone who has been through the same experience.

Mid-life and career

Women Empowerment

Since starting my business I have been co-actively empowering women. Working with women at every stage of life has been some of the most fulfilling work I have undertaken. What exactly is a women’s empowerment coach? In this role I work with women who find themselves at a crossroads in life. Leaving the workforce, Returning to the workforce, Changing professional goals, Getting married, Getting divorced, Finding oneself in mid-life, Leadership goals, Assertiveness in the work place.
These are all life course junctures during which a woman might benefit from a greater sense of self and empowerment. I work with each client to define and achieve exciting
personal goals.
I support women to: find their voice, set goals and achieve them, develop confidence and solution-centered plans, to unlock the next chapters of their lives in fulfilling ways that
bring happiness, increased self-esteem and insight into how life can be lived to the fullest.
