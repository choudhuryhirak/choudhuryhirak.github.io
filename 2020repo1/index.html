All of us go through the phase of supressing what truly drives us. We call this being «rational» Slowly and steadily we notice that we have stopped living in the need of being «rational». It feels like the rat on the hampster. All we need in such moments is to really be bold and look out for the next step to explore within with a coach - someone who has been through the same experience.
