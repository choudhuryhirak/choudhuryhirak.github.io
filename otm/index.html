OTM 2018

OTM is India’s biggest travel trade show. It is organised every year in the largest travel market in India – Mumbai.

 Why OTM 

OTM provides you the most effective reach to the most lucrative travel markets of India.

• 20.38 million Indian outbound travelers in 2015, likely to grow to 50 mn in 2020.
• International tourism expenditure by Indians likely to grow to US$ 28 bn in 2020.
• 850 mn domestic tourists, many with potential to travel abroad.
