Why Exhibit here? 

OTM Mumbai has the largest exhibiting space and the highest number of participants in any travel show in India. Mumbai is the largest travel market in the country, generating one-third (33%) of all India-outbound travellers.

Support for your presence

India’s biggest hosted buyer program was introduced at OTM in 2015. Since then, hundreds of qualified buyers are hosted every year during the OTM.
