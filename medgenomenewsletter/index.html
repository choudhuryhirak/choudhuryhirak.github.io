Summary of the talk:

As with singleton pregnancies, screening for aneuploidies non-invasively is very important for twin pregnancies.

In this webinar, you will learn about:

How NIPT can enable highly accurate screening for twin/ surrogate and egg donor pregnancies.

What additional aneuploidies can be detected using NIPT. Claria NIPT can detect the most common microdeletion syndrome, 22q11.2 and more.

How accurate determination of Zygosity can be a reliable marker for triaging twin pregnancies of conditions such as growth restriction, twin to twin transfusion syndrome.

About the speaker

Dr. Priya Kadam oversees the Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing (NIPT) services at MedGenome. She has a Master of Science by research from National University of Singapore and an MBBS from Bangalore Medical College.
